Game Mechanics
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
I   Sword   Deals a moderate damage every time,
            

II  Dagger  Has base damage of 30 but can
            be boosted by +10, +20 ... +100
            Will be boosted 1/x times
            
III Spell   Has a 1/10 chance of dealing
            160 damage, does nothing
            9/10 times

IV  Block   Reduces incoming damage by 50%, has
            a 1/x chance of blocking all damage
            and stunning enemy

V   Dodge   Has a 1/x chance of negating
            all damage

VI  Heal    If health is below maximum,
            restore full hp
